About Us

MN Transport Company-SMC LTD was incorporated in 2019 to provide livelihood for Ugandans through the provision of a Boda-Boda leasing facility.

To date the company has given out over 300 boda bodas which has positively impacted the lives of the beneficiaries.

MN Transport company also offers fleet management services to individuals who want to join the business but have no expertise or time to manage their own fleets.

Our Advantages

MN Transport Company-SMC LTD offers fleet management services to investors who wish to own Boda Bodas but have no time or expertise to manage them. The following are the services we offer under fleet management

Competence

All of our recruited riders are competent and licensed to drive

Procurement

We handle purchase of bikes and accessories

Tracking

Tracking motor bikes in and around the city

Payments

Collection and remittance of weekly payments

Payment Security

Impounding of bikes from non-paying riders

Recovery

Recovery of impounded bikes from police or other law enforcement agencies.

NOTE

The company charges a fee of only UGX 20,000 per bike per week. An investor will earn a minimum net return of 24% per year per bike which is highly competitive.
